Local veteran resources

East Tennessee veteran starting points.

Start with the resource cards and links on this site. They point to official offices, public resources and practical starting points. Check current details yourself before you drive, apply, call or make plans.

VA BenefitsState Benefits
Verify details directly with the provider. Local office hours, phone numbers, appointment rules and eligibility can change.

Mountain Home VA Medical Center

Regional VA medical center serving East Tennessee veterans.

Location: 809 Lamont Street, Mountain Home, TN 37684.

Phone: 423-926-1171.

Practical note: Bring VA ID, photo ID and current medication list. Call first for appointment rules.

Last verified: June 2026.

Tennessee Department of Veterans Services

State veteran services and benefits information.

Location: 312 Rosa L. Parks Avenue, Nashville, TN 37243.

Phone: 615-741-2345.

Practical note: Use the county VSO lookup or customer inquiry form before driving to a state office.

Last verified: June 2026.

Visit Resource

Knox County Veterans Services

Local veteran support in Knox County.

Location: Knox Central Building, 1000 North Central Street, Box 6, Knoxville, TN 37917.

Phone: 865-215-5645.

Practical note: Appointment required. Bring DD-214 and any VA claim letters.

Last verified: June 2026.
