Women veterans

Women veterans resources in East Tennessee.

Start with official VA women veterans resources and privacy-first support. Vols4Vets does not collect medical or trauma details.

Fast answer: Use VA women veterans health care resources or the Women Veterans Call Center for official routing. For crisis support, call 988 then press 1 or text 838255.

Who this page is for

Women veterans in East Tennessee, and people helping them, who need health care routing, benefits starting points, privacy-first support or official VA contacts.

First three steps

  1. Decide whether the need is health care, benefits, crisis support, records or local resources.
  2. Use an official VA women veterans resource first.
  3. Keep private health or trauma details inside official VA, medical or crisis support channels.

Official starting points

VA Women Veterans Health CareOfficial VA health care information for women veterans.Women Veterans Call CenterOfficial VA call, text and chat routing for women veterans.VA MST supportOfficial VA support information. Use official channels for private details.

What to prepare before calling

What kind of help is needed, county, safe contact method, whether VA health care is already set up and any appointment or deadline information.

Common mistakes

  • Sharing sensitive medical or trauma details with a non-medical website.
  • Assuming a general office is the right contact for women veteran health questions.
  • Waiting to ask for help because records are not organized.

Safety and privacy note

Vols4Vets is not a medical provider or crisis line. Do not send medical records, private trauma details, claim numbers, DD214s, passwords or private case details to Vols4Vets.
