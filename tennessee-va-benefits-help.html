Tennessee benefits

VA benefits help in Tennessee.

A safe starting point for sorting federal VA benefits, Tennessee benefits and accredited claims help.

Fast answer: Use VA.gov for federal benefits, Tennessee Department of Veterans Services for state programs and the VA accredited representative search for claims help. Vols4Vets does not file or manage claims.

Who this page is for

Tennessee veterans, survivors, spouses and caregivers who need to know where to start without sharing private claim details with Vols4Vets.

First three steps

  1. Decide whether the question is federal VA benefits, Tennessee state benefits or local county help.
  2. Use the official source below that matches the question.
  3. For claim-specific help, use an accredited representative, VSO, attorney or claims agent.

Official starting points

VA.govFederal VA benefits, health care, records and account access.Tennessee state veteran benefitsOfficial Tennessee state benefits starting point.Find a VA accredited representative or VSOOfficial VA search for accredited claims help.

Before you call or drive

  • Check the official page.
  • Confirm hours.
  • Confirm appointment rules.
  • Ask what documents to bring.
  • Ask whether copies are accepted.
  • Ask whether the office handles your exact issue.
  • Do not send private records to Vols4Vets.

What to bring

DD214 if available, photo ID, VA letters, marriage or dependent records if relevant and a list of questions. Use official portals for direct deposit or private claim details.

What not to send to Vols4Vets

Do not send Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, legal papers, passwords or private case details.

Common mistakes

  • Mixing up Tennessee state benefits with federal VA benefits.
  • Paying for claims help before checking accredited or free options.
  • Sharing private details with an unverified person or website.

When to use urgent or crisis help

Benefits questions are not emergencies. If there is immediate danger, call 911. If a veteran is in crisis, call 988 then press 1 or text 838255.

Source note
