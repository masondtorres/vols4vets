Sevier County VSO

Sevier County Veteran Service Office guide.

Use the official county page first, then bring the right questions and documents. Vols4Vets is independent and does not file claims.

Back to East Tennessee resources
Fast answer: The official Sevier County Veterans Services page lists the office at 1124 Blanton Drive, Suite 101, Sevierville, TN 37862, phone 865-453-8073, and Monday-Friday hours of 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. with lunch closure from 11:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. Check that official page before you call or drive.

Who this page is for

Veterans, survivors, family members and caregivers in Sevier County who need help finding the right official starting point for VA benefits, state benefits, records or related paperwork.

Official starting point

Sevier County Veterans ServicesOfficial county page for current office details.TDVS county office lookupOfficial Tennessee lookup for county veterans services offices.VA accredited representative searchOfficial VA search for accredited claims help.

What this office or resource may help with

The county office may help veterans and families understand benefit paperwork, prepare for official VA or state benefit steps, and identify the right office for the issue. Confirm the exact service directly with the office.

What to check before you call or drive

Check the official office page before you call or drive. Hours, appointment rules and locations can change.

Before you call or drive

  • Check the official page.
  • Confirm hours.
  • Confirm appointment rules.
  • Ask what documents to bring.
  • Ask whether copies are accepted.
  • Ask whether the office handles your exact issue.
  • Do not send private records to Vols4Vets.

What to bring

Photo ID, DD214 if available, VA letters, current mailing address, dependent or marriage records if relevant, copies of records already requested by the office, and a short written list of questions.

What not to send to Vols4Vets

Do not send Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, legal papers, passwords or private case details to Vols4Vets.

What happens after this link

  • You may leave Vols4Vets.
  • The official site may ask for private information.
  • Only enter private records on official secure sites or with verified accredited help.
  • Write down confirmation numbers.
  • Save copies of what you submit.
  • Come back to Vols4Vets if you need the next starting point.

Common mistakes

  • Driving over without checking lunch closure or appointment rules.
  • Assuming Vols4Vets can review claim evidence or represent you.
  • Sending private records through normal email before an official office asks for them.
