Guidelines

Community guidelines.

Vols4Vets is not launching a forum now. These rules define what would be required before any community feature exists.

No forum is active. This page is a future-facing policy only.

Core rules

  • No medical advice.
  • No legal advice.
  • No claims advice.
  • No harassment or threats.
  • No private records.
  • No doxxing or posting someone else's private details.

Crisis escalation

If someone appears in immediate danger, the right action is emergency or crisis support, not community discussion.

Moderation rules

Future community content would need human moderation, clear removal rules and a way to report unsafe content before launch.

Last reviewed: July 1, 2026.
