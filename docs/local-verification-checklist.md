# Local Verification Checklist

Use this before adding any phone number, office, business, nonprofit or local provider to Vols4Vets.

- Official website verified.
- Phone number verified.
- Address verified.
- Hours verified.
- Appointment requirement verified.
- Eligibility rules verified.
- Cost or fee details checked.
- Service area checked.
- No partnership implied.
- No endorsement implied.
- No private records collected by Vols4Vets.
- No Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, legal papers, passwords or private case details requested by Vols4Vets.
- Sponsor or affiliate relationship reviewed.
- Risk level assigned.
- Last verified date added.
- Verification method recorded.
- Correction path exists.
- Next review date added.
