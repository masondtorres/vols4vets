# Action Plan Tool Notes

## What Find My Next Step Does

Find My Next Step asks five client-side questions and returns a starting path, three next steps, what to bring, what not to send, official starting links and related Vols4Vets pages.

## What It Does Not Do

It does not diagnose, file claims, give legal advice, give medical advice, contact an office, monitor emergencies or submit information to Vols4Vets.

## localStorage Privacy Choice

Saved paths use localStorage only. Saved paths stay in the browser on the user's device and are not sent to Vols4Vets. Users can clear the saved path from the tool.

## Client-Side-Only Behavior

The tool has no backend, account, payment, login, form submission or database.

## Safety Limitations

The tool is a starting-point router. Crisis and immediate danger copy points to 911 and 988 press 1. Private records should stay with official secure sites or verified accredited help.
