# Partnership and Sponsor Risk Rules

## Core Boundaries

- No implied partnerships.
- No pay-to-rank resource listings.
- No sponsor placement on crisis pages.
- No sponsor claims that weaken official-resource-first routing.

## Private Records Risk

Do not list or promote sponsors that collect private records without clear privacy review. Resources that request Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, legal papers or private case details need extra review.

## Correction Process

Correction requests should be checked against official sources before public changes are made.

## Disclosure Rules

If a future sponsorship exists, disclose it near the listing and keep ranking independent from payment.

## Approval Checklist

- Official website checked.
- Privacy behavior reviewed.
- No crisis-page placement.
- No claims of endorsement or affiliation.
- No private-record collection without clear official need.
