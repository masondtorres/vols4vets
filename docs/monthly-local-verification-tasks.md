# Monthly Local Verification Tasks

Run this checklist monthly before updating local office pages or submitting sitemap changes.

## Link and Route Checks

- Check all local office guide links for broken pages or redirects.
- Confirm each clean URL still appears in vercel.json and sitemap.xml.
- Confirm removed ScreenTools and utility cleanup redirects are not listed in sitemap.xml.

## Office Detail Checks

- Check for changed office hours on official county, TDVS, VA, American Job Center and Legal Aid pages.
- Check for changed phone numbers only from official sources.
- Check for changed appointment rules, walk-in rules, lunch closures and remote consultation notes.
- Check for new official county links or updated county pages.
- Remove or revise programs that are closed, renamed or no longer listed by the official source.

## Safety and Corrections

- Review correction requests and update only after verifying against an official source.
- Keep warnings against sending Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, legal papers, passwords or private case details to Vols4Vets.
- Re-scan for unsafe claims such as guaranteed, approved, official partner, legal advice or medical advice.

## Search and Indexing

- Review resources-data.js for stale titles, tags and last reviewed dates.
- Review sitemap.xml after every local office update.
- Check Google Search Console indexing for the local office guide pages and request indexing when a useful page is updated.
- Watch for crawled old utility URLs and keep them routed to the cleanup page, not sitemap.xml.
