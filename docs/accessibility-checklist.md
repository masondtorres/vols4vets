# Accessibility Checklist

Use this checklist for static Vols4Vets HTML/CSS/JS changes.

## Keyboard And Focus

- Keyboard navigation reaches major links, buttons and form controls.
- Focus states are visible on links, buttons, inputs, selects and textareas.
- Skip link works and moves focus to main content.
- Mobile nav toggle is keyboard accessible.

## Forms And Buttons

- Question groups use `fieldset` and `legend`.
- Radio and checkbox labels are clickable.
- Buttons use `type="button"` unless they submit a form.
- Submit buttons are clear and only submit client-side behavior where intended.

## Structure

- Heading order is logical.
- Breadcrumbs do not replace the page heading.
- Alt text exists for meaningful images.
- Decorative image alt text is empty when appropriate.
- No instruction depends on color alone.

## Readability

- Contrast remains readable.
- Tap targets are reasonably sized on mobile.
- Printed action plans hide navigation and page chrome while keeping plan text readable.
- Crisis copy remains visible in HTML and in generated crisis-path output.

## Safety And Privacy

- No form asks for private records.
- No page asks users to send Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, legal papers, passwords, private case details, medical history, legal history or claim evidence.
- Feedback and weekly update pages remain placeholders until a safe provider and privacy terms exist.
