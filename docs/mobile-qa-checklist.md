# Mobile QA Checklist

Use this before publishing major Vols4Vets content or navigation changes.

## Devices And Browsers

- iPhone Safari check.
- Android Chrome check.
- Small screen check around 320px wide.

## Navigation

- Skip link appears when focused.
- Mobile nav opens with keyboard and touch.
- Mobile nav closes when the toggle is pressed again.
- Nav links are readable and large enough to tap.
- Footer links are reachable and readable.

## Find My Next Step

- Five questions are visible before JavaScript enhancement.
- Action plan result generates.
- Print button is visible.
- Copy button works or shows the fallback message.
- Save path works on this device only.
- Clear saved path works.
- Start over clears the visible result and scrolls back to the form.
- Crisis path produces 911 and 988 language.
- No-JS fallback is visible when JavaScript is off.
- No data is submitted to a server.

## Key Pages

- Search page works on mobile.
- Site map page is readable.
- Top five toolkit pages are readable:
  - VA Claim Starter Checklist.
  - Housing Risk Action Checklist.
  - Veteran Job Search Starter Kit.
  - DD214 Replacement Checklist.
  - County Office Call Script.
- Privacy warnings are visible on toolkit and feedback pages.

## Safety

- Do not add analytics, pixels, cookies or private-data collection during mobile QA.
- Do not ask users for Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, legal papers, passwords, private case details, medical history, legal history or claim evidence.
