# Outreach

## Targets

County veteran offices, VA contacts, local nonprofits, veteran-owned businesses, churches, VFW posts, American Legion posts, workforce offices, housing organizations, legal aid organizations, women veteran groups and caregiver groups.

## Official Office Or County Office Verification

Subject: Verify veteran resource information for Vols4Vets

Hello,

Vols4Vets is updating a plain-language veteran resource guide for East Tennessee. We are not asking for an endorsement. We only want to verify public information so veterans and families start in the right place.

Can you confirm the best public phone number, website, hours, service area, appointment rules and what documents a veteran should bring?

Please do not send private case details, Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s or medical records.

Thank you.

## Nonprofit, Church Or Workforce Office Verification

Subject: Quick public-info check for a veteran resource listing

Hello,

We are checking public resource details for Vols4Vets. We are not asking for an endorsement or partnership. We want to avoid sending veterans to outdated phone numbers or wrong doors.

Can you confirm your public contact information, who qualifies, county or city served, any costs, whether appointments are required and what a veteran should bring?

Please share only public program information.

## Veteran-Owned Business Listing Verification

Subject: Verify public details for a veteran-owned business listing

Hello,

Vols4Vets is preparing a local veteran-owned business listing. This is not an endorsement. We are verifying public information before anything is listed.

Can you confirm business name, public website, public phone, city, category, veteran ownership status, license or accreditation if relevant, and whether any military discount is current?

Sponsored placements must be labeled. Veteran-owned does not automatically mean trusted.
