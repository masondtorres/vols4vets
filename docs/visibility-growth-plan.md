# Visibility Growth Plan

## Rules

Use white-hat SEO only. Do not buy spam backlinks, create fake reviews, invent authority, stuff keywords or imply affiliation.

## Long-Tail Keyword Clusters

- VA claim checklist, claim starter, accredited representative
- DD214 replacement and military records
- Sevier County VSO and Knox County VSO
- Mountain Home VA appointment prep
- Housing risk, eviction checklist and homeless veteran support
- Veteran job search starter and military skills resume
- Women veterans and MST support
- Family caregiver and Military OneSource

## Internal Link Plan

Link from homepage to Find My Next Step and core toolkits. Link from resources to toolkit and local office guides. Link each toolkit to official pages and related Vols4Vets pages.

## Social Posting Ideas

- One checklist per post.
- One official resource reminder per week.
- One county verification update when manually confirmed.

## County-by-County Future Expansion

Add counties only when official office details are verified and maintenance capacity exists.

## Manual Metrics

Track Search Console impressions, indexed pages, top queries, correction requests, broken links and Vercel analytics if enabled.
