Housing crisis

Homeless veteran help in East Tennessee.

If housing is unstable, start with urgent official support. Vols4Vets cannot promise shelter placement and does not collect eviction papers.

Fast answer: If there is immediate danger, call 911. For veteran crisis support, call 988 then press 1 or text 838255. For homeless veteran support, contact the VA National Call Center for Homeless Veterans.

Who this page is for

Veterans and helpers in East Tennessee who are homeless, staying somewhere temporary, facing eviction, living in a vehicle or worried housing may be lost soon.

First three steps

  1. Use 911 first if there is immediate danger.
  2. Contact VA homeless veteran support if the person is homeless or at risk of homelessness.
  3. Write down county, safe callback option, deadline, pets or dependents and any immediate safety issue.

Official starting points

VA National Call Center for Homeless VeteransOfficial VA 24/7 homeless veteran support.VA Homeless ProgramsOfficial VA housing and homeless veteran program information.Legal Aid of East TennesseeCivil legal help starting point for eligible residents.

What to prepare before calling

County, current sleeping situation, immediate safety concerns, safe contact method, eviction or utility deadline, ID status and whether DD214 is available. Do not delay the call if records are missing.

Common mistakes

  • Waiting until the day of eviction to ask for help.
  • Assuming one call guarantees shelter placement.
  • Emailing private legal papers or case details to Vols4Vets.

Safety and privacy note

Vols4Vets is not an emergency service, shelter provider, law firm or case manager. Do not send eviction papers, Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, legal papers, passwords or private case details to Vols4Vets.
