Mountain Home VA

Mountain Home VA location checklist.

Use VA.gov location pages before you drive. Services, clinic hours and appointment rules may vary by department.

Fast answer: The official VA location page lists James H. Quillen VA Medical Center at Corner of Lamont Street and Veterans Way, Mountain Home, TN 37684, with facility hours shown as 24/7 and service hours varying by service. Check the official VA page before you travel.

Who this page is for

Veterans and caregivers checking whether Mountain Home VA, a VA clinic, Vet Center or another VA location is the right place for care, benefits routing or records questions.

Official starting point

James H. Quillen VA Medical CenterOfficial VA location page.VA Find LocationsOfficial VA tool for facilities, clinics, Vet Centers and offices.VA Mountain Home health careOfficial VA health care system page.

What this office or resource may help with

Mountain Home VA may provide health care and related VA services listed on VA.gov. Service-specific appointments, referrals and hours may differ from general facility hours.

What to check before you call or drive

Check the official VA page before you call or drive. Hours, appointment rules and locations can change. Confirm the exact clinic, building, parking, service hours and whether a referral is needed.

Before you call or drive

  • Check the official page.
  • Confirm hours.
  • Confirm appointment rules.
  • Ask what documents to bring.
  • Ask whether copies are accepted.
  • Ask whether the office handles your exact issue.
  • Do not send private records to Vols4Vets.

What to bring

Photo ID, VA health card if you have one, appointment details, medication list, insurance cards if any, and records the VA specifically asks you to bring.

What not to send to Vols4Vets

Do not send medical records, claim numbers, DD214s, legal papers, passwords or private case details to Vols4Vets.

Common mistakes

  • Going to the main campus when the service is at a clinic or different VA office.
  • Assuming 24/7 facility hours mean every clinic or service is open.
  • Using Vols4Vets for medical questions instead of VA or emergency channels.
