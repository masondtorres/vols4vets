Sevierville

Veteran resources for Sevierville.

Use this page when you are in Sevierville and need to decide which official office or public resource to contact first.

Fast answer: For Sevierville, start with Sevier County routing for local questions, VA.gov for federal benefits and records, VA Find Locations for care and Tennessee veterans services for state benefits.

Who this page is for

Veterans, service members, spouses, caregivers and family members in Sevierville who need a first step for benefits, records, housing risk, jobs, health care or family support.

First three steps

  1. Decide if the problem is urgent. If yes, use crisis or emergency support first.
  2. Check whether your issue is county, state or federal before calling.
  3. Ask the official office what to bring and whether an appointment is required.

Official starting points

VA.govFederal VA benefits, records and health care starting point.Tennessee Department of Veterans ServicesStatewide benefits and veterans services routing.VA Find LocationsFind VA clinics, hospitals, Vet Centers and offices.

What to bring

Photo ID, DD214 if available, VA letters, proof of address if requested, housing notices if relevant and a short list of questions.

What not to send to Vols4Vets

Do not send private records, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, passwords or private case details to Vols4Vets.

Common mistakes

  • Calling a federal VA number for a county-only question.
  • Missing office hours or appointment rules.
  • Sending sensitive paperwork to a general email inbox.

When to use urgent or crisis help

Call 911 for immediate danger. Call 988 then press 1 or text 838255 for veteran crisis support. For homelessness or imminent housing loss, use VA homeless veteran support first.
