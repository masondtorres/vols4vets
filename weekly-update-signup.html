Weekly updates

Get one useful veteran resource update.

Send a simple email request to be added to the Vols4Vets weekly update list. Do not include private records or case details.

Request weekly updatesMonthly update log
Fast answer: Click the signup button. Your email app will open with a prepared request. Send it from the address you want added.

What updates include

  • New Vols4Vets checklist pages.
  • Monthly source review notes.
  • New official-resource guides.
  • Search and sitemap cleanup updates.

What Vols4Vets will never ask for by email

  • No Social Security numbers.
  • No claim numbers.
  • No DD214s.
  • No medical records.
  • No legal papers.
  • No passwords.
  • No private case details.

Manual check option

You can still use the monthly log, toolkit hub, resources page or site map without joining the update list.

Privacy note

This signup path uses your email app. Do not send private records. Vols4Vets will use the email address only for site updates unless you ask for something else.
