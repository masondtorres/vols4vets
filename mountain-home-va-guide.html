VA care

Mountain Home VA starting guide.

Use official VA tools to confirm locations, clinic services, eligibility, appointments, hours and directions before you drive.

Fast answer: For Mountain Home VA, start with the official VA Find Locations page and VA health care pages. Call the official number listed by VA before traveling for care or paperwork.

First three steps

  1. Use VA Find Locations to confirm the correct facility, clinic or service.
  2. Check whether you need an appointment, referral, enrollment or a specific clinic entrance.
  3. Bring your ID, appointment details and a list of current medications or questions.

What to bring

Photo ID, VA health card if you have one, appointment letter or portal details, current medication list, insurance cards if any, and records the official VA office specifically asks for.

Common mistakes

  • Driving to the wrong VA location for a specialty service.
  • Assuming walk-ins are available for every need.
  • Sending medical records to Vols4Vets instead of using official VA portals.

Related Vols4Vets pages

East Tennessee resources, Tennessee VA benefits help, VA benefits and health care resources.

Safety and privacy note

Vols4Vets is not a medical provider and does not schedule VA care. If there is immediate danger, call 911. Do not send medical records, passwords or private case details to Vols4Vets.
