VA care

Mountain Home VA starting guide.

Use official VA tools to confirm services, appointments, eligibility, locations and directions before you drive.

Fast answer: Start with VA Find Locations and VA health care pages. Vols4Vets does not schedule care or verify appointments.

Who this page is for

East Tennessee veterans and helpers trying to figure out whether Mountain Home VA, a VA clinic, a Vet Center or another VA location is the right next step.

First three steps

  1. Use VA Find Locations to confirm the correct VA facility or office.
  2. Check whether the service needs an appointment, referral or enrollment.
  3. Bring your ID, appointment details and a short list of questions.

Official starting points

VA Find LocationsOfficial VA tool for hospitals, clinics, Vet Centers and offices.VA health careApply for, manage and learn about VA health care.VA urgent care informationOfficial VA explanation of urgent care access.

What to bring

Photo ID, VA health card if you have one, appointment information, current medication list, insurance cards if any and records the official VA office specifically requests.

What not to send to Vols4Vets

Do not send medical records, passwords, claim numbers or private case details. Use secure official VA channels for care information.

Common mistakes

  • Going to the wrong VA location for a specialty service.
  • Assuming walk-ins are available for every need.
  • Sending medical information to a non-medical website.

When to use urgent or crisis help

If there is immediate danger, call 911. For veteran crisis support, call 988 then press 1 or text 838255. Use official VA urgent care information for non-life-threatening urgent care questions.

Related Vols4Vets pages

East Tennessee resources, Tennessee VA benefits help, VA benefits and health care resources.

Source note

VA locations, clinic services and hours can change. Check VA Find Locations before you drive or call.
