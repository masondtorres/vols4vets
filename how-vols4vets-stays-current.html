Trust

How Vols4Vets stays current.

Vols4Vets uses official-source-first review and tells users to verify before travel.

Official-source-first policy

Vols4Vets starts with official government, VA, county, workforce, legal aid or direct provider pages when possible.

Monthly review

Local pages should be checked monthly for broken links, changed hours, phone numbers, appointment rules and removed programs.

Correction requests

Correction requests are reviewed against official sources before a page is changed.

Local verification checklist

Vols4Vets tracks what has and has not been verified in local documentation before listing exact details.

Weekly update status

Weekly email updates are not active yet. See the weekly update signup status page before sending anything to Vols4Vets.

Feedback status

No feedback form is active yet. See Feedback Guidelines for safe future feedback rules.

What is not verified yet

Some local phone numbers, office hours, appointment rules and intake paths still need Mason or Jen to confirm manually before publication.

Verify before travel

Check the official office page before you call or drive. Hours, locations and eligibility can change.

Last reviewed: July 1, 2026.
