Boundaries

No inflated promises.

Vols4Vets is an independent veteran-owned site. It is not the VA, not a law firm, not a benefits office and not an emergency service. We do not guarantee benefits, claims, discounts or outcomes.

Use the source first

Official offices, VA clinics, state resources, local providers and public pages should be your first stop. Check hours, eligibility, location and current rules before you act.

Simple process

Three steps. No runaround.

1

Start with the directory

Choose the topic that fits your need: crisis help, VA benefits, jobs, family support, local help or discounts.

2

Go to the official source

Use the linked provider, office or public resource. Do not rely on screenshots or old social media posts.

3

Contact only if needed

Vols4Vets is a small veteran-owned site, not a staffed help desk. Use the About page contact section only as a last resort.
