Feedback Guidelines

No feedback form is active. This page explains how safe one-question feedback may work later.

Fast answer: Vols4Vets may later ask one question: “Did this give you a clear next step?” Do not send private case details.

What feedback may ask later

A future prompt may ask whether a page gave you a clear next step. It should not ask for private records, medical history, legal history or claim evidence.

What not to send

  • No private case details.
  • No claim numbers.
  • No DD214s.
  • No medical records.
  • No legal papers.
  • No emergency messages.
  • No Social Security numbers, passwords, medical history, legal history or claim evidence.

Where to go instead

Use crisis support or 911 for emergencies. Use Correction Request for public-resource errors. Use official offices, secure official portals or verified accredited help for private records.
