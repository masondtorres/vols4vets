Jobs and training

Veteran job help in East Tennessee.

Start with official workforce resources for resumes, training, certifications, apprenticeships and federal hiring. Vols4Vets has no employer partnerships and cannot promise employment.

Fast answer: Use Tennessee workforce and American Job Center starting points first. Bring a resume, work history, military training and a list of job targets.

Who this page is for

Veterans, service members and spouses in East Tennessee looking for job search help, resume translation, training, certification options, apprenticeships or federal job starting points.

First three steps

  1. Write down the job type, schedule, pay range and location you need.
  2. Gather resume, military job history, certifications and training records.
  3. Use official workforce resources and ask about veteran services.

Official starting points

Tennessee Virtual American Job Center veterans programVeteran job services, training information, counseling and resume preparation.Find a Tennessee American Job CenterOfficial local job center finder.USAJOBS veterans hiring informationFederal hiring starting point for veterans.

Before you call or drive

  • Check the official page.
  • Confirm hours.
  • Confirm appointment rules.
  • Ask what documents to bring.
  • Ask whether copies are accepted.
  • Ask whether the office handles your exact issue.
  • Do not send private records to Vols4Vets.

What to prepare before calling

Resume, dates of service, MOS/AFSC/rate, civilian job targets, certifications, training records, education history and barriers such as transportation or schedule limits.

What happens after this link

  • You may leave Vols4Vets.
  • The official site may ask for private information.
  • Only enter private records on official secure sites or with verified accredited help.
  • Write down confirmation numbers.
  • Save copies of what you submit.
  • Come back to Vols4Vets if you need the next starting point.

Common mistakes

  • Using military acronyms without translating duties.
  • Applying everywhere without a target job type.
  • Believing anyone can promise a job or interview.

Safety and privacy note

Do not send Social Security numbers, passwords, private case details or identity documents to Vols4Vets. Use official workforce portals for applications and records.
