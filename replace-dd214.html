Military records

How to replace a DD214.

Use official records request channels. Vols4Vets does not accept DD214 uploads or private military records.

Fast answer: Start with official VA records pages or the National Archives military records request process. Use secure official portals for private records.

First three steps

  1. Confirm whether you need a DD214, service treatment records, VA records or another military record.
  2. Use the official request process and read identity verification instructions carefully.
  3. Save confirmation numbers and keep copies of any request you submit.

What to bring

Full legal name used during service, branch, approximate service dates, date of birth, current mailing address and any official request information. Only provide sensitive details through official secure channels.

Common mistakes

  • Uploading DD214s to unofficial sites.
  • Requesting the wrong record type.
  • Waiting until the day before an appointment or deadline.

Related Vols4Vets pages

what to bring to a VSO, Tennessee VA benefits help, start a VA disability claim.

Safety and privacy note

Do not send DD214s, Social Security numbers, claim numbers, medical records, legal papers, passwords or private case details to Vols4Vets. Use official VA or National Archives channels for records requests.
