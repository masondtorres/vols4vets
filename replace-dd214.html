Military records

How to replace a DD214.

Use official records request channels. Vols4Vets does not accept DD214 uploads or private military records.

Fast answer: Start with the National Archives or VA records page. Most veterans and next of kin can request free copies of DD214s through official channels.

Who this page is for

Veterans, next of kin and helpers who need a replacement DD214 or other service records for benefits, burial, employment, school or personal records.

First three steps

  1. Confirm whether you need a DD214, full service record, medical record or VA record.
  2. Use an official request channel and follow identity verification instructions.
  3. Save confirmation numbers and keep copies of request details.

Official starting points

National Archives military service recordsOfficial records request starting point.VA military service records pageVA guide to requesting DD214 and other records.National Archives DD214 pageBackground and records center information.

What to bring

Full legal name used during service, branch, approximate service dates, date of birth, current mailing address and any official request confirmation. Use official secure channels for sensitive details.

What not to send to Vols4Vets

Do not send DD214s, Social Security numbers, claim numbers, medical records, legal papers, passwords or private case details.

What happens after this link

  • You may leave Vols4Vets.
  • The official site may ask for private information.
  • Only enter private records on official secure sites or with verified accredited help.
  • Write down confirmation numbers.
  • Save copies of what you submit.
  • Come back to Vols4Vets if you need the next starting point.

Common mistakes

  • Uploading DD214s to unofficial sites.
  • Requesting the wrong record type.
  • Waiting until the day before an appointment or deadline.

When to use urgent or crisis help

A records request is usually not emergency help. If there is immediate danger, call 911. For veteran crisis support, call 988 then press 1 or text 838255.
