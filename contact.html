Contact

Use resources first. Contact only if needed.

Vols4Vets is a small veteran-owned site, not a staffed help desk. The protected contact section is on the About page to reduce spam and bot scraping.

Before you reach out

Use the directory first. If you still need to contact Vols4Vets, include your East Tennessee county, the kind of resource you need and how to reply.

Do not send Social Security numbers, claim numbers, medical records or passwords.

This is not a crisis line.

If you or someone you know is in crisis, call 988 and press 1 or text 838255. For immediate danger, call 911.
