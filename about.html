About Us

By Veterans. For Veterans.

We are a veteran-owned LLC based in Sevierville, Tennessee — the heart of the Volunteer State.

Born from the same spirit that built the University of Tennessee Vols and the rugged hills of East Tennessee, we stand with our brothers and sisters who answered the call.

The uniform comes off, but the mission never ends. Vols4Vets is here to help with real support — no bureaucracy, no empty words, just action from veterans who understand.
