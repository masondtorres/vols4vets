About Vols4Vets

Service did not end when the uniform came off.

Vols4Vets is a veteran-owned resource and connection organization serving Sevierville and East Tennessee.

Our founders

Mason and Jen Torres

Mason and Jen are both United States Air Force veterans. They have lived in East Tennessee since 1998 and raised their family here.

They built Vols4Vets because veterans and families need direct starting points, not another pile of links with no order.

Authority here comes from lived experience. The goal is simple. Help the next veteran find the next right step.

Local roots

Vols4Vets is an independent veteran-owned LLC based in Sevierville, Tennessee. We help veterans and families find clearer next steps.

Mission

Clear direction for East Tennessee veterans.

Vols4Vets gathers useful resources, highlights local contacts and points veterans toward responsible providers.

Useful resources

Benefits, health care, crisis support, jobs, housing, education and family support.

Local connection

Sevier County, Knoxville, Mountain Home VA and East Tennessee offices that can be contacted directly.

Straight answers

We say what we are, what we are not and where another organization is a better fit.

What Vols4Vets is

Vols4Vets is an independent veteran-owned LLC based in Sevierville, Tennessee. We help veterans and families find clearer next steps.

If we can point you toward a useful next step, we will. If another organization is a better fit, we will tell you that too.

What Vols4Vets is not

We are not the Department of Veterans Affairs, a crisis line, a law firm or an accredited claims representative. For immediate danger call 911. For crisis support call 988 and press 1.
