About Vols4Vets

Service did not end when the uniform came off.

Vols4Vets is a veteran-owned LLC built to help East Tennessee veterans and military families find practical resources and local connection.

Our founders

Mason and Jen Torres

Mason and Jen are both United States Air Force veterans. They have lived in East Tennessee since 1998 and raised their family here.

They understand that leaving military service does not end the need for trusted people, clear direction and practical support. Vols4Vets was created to make useful starting points easier to find.

East Tennessee is home.

Vols4Vets is based in Sevierville and focuses first on the veterans, service members and military families who live across East Tennessee.

Our mission

Help veterans find a clearer next step

We gather useful resources, highlight local options and connect people with the organizations best equipped to help.

Useful resources

Clear starting points for benefits, health care, jobs, housing, family support and more.

Local direction

East Tennessee offices, clinics and organizations that veterans can contact directly.

Straight answers

No claim that Vols4Vets replaces the VA, accredited representatives or emergency services.

What Vols4Vets is

An independent, veteran-owned resource and connection organization serving East Tennessee.

What Vols4Vets is not

Vols4Vets is not the Department of Veterans Affairs, a crisis service, a law firm or an accredited claims representative.
