About Vols4Vets

Clear starting points for East Tennessee veterans.

Vols4Vets is a veteran-owned resource site based in Sevierville. The goal is simple: help veterans and families find the next useful step without digging through clutter.

Founders

Mason and Jen Torres

Mason and Jen are United States Air Force veterans. They have lived in East Tennessee since 1998 and raised their family here.

Vols4Vets exists because veterans need direct starting points, not another pile of links with no order.

Local roots

Independent. Veteran-owned. Based in Sevierville, Tennessee.

What this site does

It points you to the right starting place.

Find resources

Benefits, health care, crisis support, jobs, housing, discounts and family support.

Start local

Sevier County, Knoxville, Mountain Home VA and East Tennessee offices.

Check the source

Use official offices and providers before you drive, apply, call or make plans.

What Vols4Vets is

A small veteran-owned resource site that helps veterans and families find clearer next steps.

What Vols4Vets is not

Not the VA. Not a crisis line. Not a law firm. Not an accredited claims representative. For immediate danger call 911. For crisis support call 988 and press 1.

Contact

Contact details are protected.

To cut down on spam and bot scraping, direct contact details are not published as call or email links. Reveal them, then copy them manually.

Do not send Social Security numbers, claim numbers, medical records or passwords.

Most people should start with Resources.

The directory is faster than waiting for a reply.
