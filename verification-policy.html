Verification Policy

Vols4Vets points people to useful starting points. We do not control listed offices, programs or providers, and details can change.

We prefer official sources first. When possible, resources show a last verified date. Always confirm hours, eligibility, costs and deadlines directly before you drive, apply or call.

Verification levels

  • Level 1: official website checked.
  • Level 2: official website and phone checked.
  • Level 3: office or provider confirmed details directly.
  • Level 4: ongoing partner or trusted local source.

Correction requests are reviewed by a human. Sponsors cannot buy verified status.
