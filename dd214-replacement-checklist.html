Records toolkit

DD214 replacement checklist.

Use National Archives or official records channels. Vols4Vets does not accept DD214 uploads.

Fast answer: Start with National Archives or VA records pages and use official identity verification steps.

Who this is for

Veterans, next of kin and helpers who need a DD214 or other military service records.

First three steps

  1. Confirm whether you need a DD214, full service record or medical record.
  2. Use an official records request channel.
  3. Save confirmation numbers and copies of request details.

What to gather

Full legal name used during service, branch, approximate service dates, date of birth, current mailing address and any official request confirmation.

What not to send to Vols4Vets

Do not send Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, legal papers, passwords or private case details to Vols4Vets.

Official starting points

National Archives military service recordsOfficial records request starting point.National Archives DD214 pageOfficial DD214 records information.

What happens after this link

  • You may leave Vols4Vets.
  • The official site may ask for private information.
  • Only enter private records on official secure sites or with verified accredited help.
  • Write down confirmation numbers.
  • Save copies of what you submit.
  • Come back to Vols4Vets if you need the next starting point.

Common mistakes

  • Uploading DD214s to unofficial sites.
  • Requesting the wrong record type.
  • Waiting until the day before an appointment.
