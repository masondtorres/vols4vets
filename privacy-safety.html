Privacy and Safety

Vols4Vets does not need private records to point users to public resources. Do not send private documents through normal email.

Use official VA, state, county, legal or medical portals for private details. Vols4Vets may collect basic form information only when users submit it. Vols4Vets does not sell private user information.

Emergency requests should not go through this site. If there is immediate danger, call 911. For veteran crisis support, call 988 then press 1 or text 838255.
