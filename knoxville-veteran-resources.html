Knoxville

Veteran resources for Knoxville.

A city-focused starting point for Knoxville veterans and families who need benefits, health care, work, housing, legal aid or records help.

Fast answer: For Knoxville, use VA.gov and VA Find Locations for VA issues, Tennessee workforce resources for job help, Legal Aid of East Tennessee for civil legal help and VA homeless support for housing crisis.

Who this page is for

Veterans, service members, spouses, caregivers and helpers in Knoxville who need to find the right starting point without sharing private records online.

First three steps

  1. Separate urgent safety needs from paperwork or appointment needs.
  2. Use the official source that fits the problem: VA, workforce, legal aid, homeless support or county routing.
  3. Confirm hours, eligibility and appointment rules before you drive.

Official starting points

VA Find LocationsFind VA care and offices near Knoxville.Tennessee veteran workforce helpResume, training and career support through workforce channels.Legal Aid of East TennesseeCivil legal help starting point for eligible East Tennessee residents.

What to bring

Photo ID, DD214 if available, VA letters, resume or work history for job help, housing or court notices if relevant and questions written in plain language.

What not to send to Vols4Vets

Do not send Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, legal papers, passwords or private case details.

Common mistakes

  • Assuming every office accepts walk-ins.
  • Using an old phone number from a search result.
  • Sending legal or medical documents to a non-official inbox.

When to use urgent or crisis help

Call 911 for immediate danger. Call 988 then press 1 or text 838255 for veteran crisis support. If you are homeless or may lose housing soon, contact VA homeless support first.
