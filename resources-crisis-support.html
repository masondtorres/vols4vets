Urgent support for veterans.

Immediate starting points for veterans, service members and families who need help now.

Call 988Text 838255
Use emergency help first. If there is immediate danger, call 911. For veteran support, call 988 then press 1 or text 838255. Vols4Vets does not monitor emergencies.

Veterans Crisis Line

24/7 support for veterans, service members and families.

988 then press 1. Text 838255.

Location: United States, 24/7 national crisis support.

Phone: 988 then press 1. Text 838255.

Practical note: Use this first for veteran crisis support. You do not need to be enrolled in VA care.

Last verified: June 2026.

988 Lifeline

24/7 support in the United States.

988

Location: United States, 24/7 national hotline.

Phone: 988. Call, text or chat.

Practical note: Use for emotional distress, suicide concerns, substance use concerns or when someone needs to talk now.

Last verified: June 2026.

National Call Center for Homeless Veterans

Help for veterans who are homeless or at risk of homelessness.

877-424-3838

Location: United States, 24/7 VA homeless veteran support.

Phone: 877-424-3838.

Practical note: Call if housing is unstable, eviction is close or temporary housing is ending.

Last verified: June 2026.

Military OneSource

Counseling, family support and military life resources.

800-342-9647

Location: Online and phone support for eligible military community members.

Phone: 800-342-9647.

Practical note: Ask about eligibility before sharing details. Secure live chat is also available.

Last verified: June 2026.

Coaching Into Care

Help for families encouraging a veteran to seek support.

888-823-7458

Location: VA online resource. Service page now redirects callers to caregiver support resources.

Phone: 888-823-7458 redirects to the Caregiver Support Line.

Practical note: Verify the current VA page before relying on this service. Use 988 then press 1 for crisis support.

Last verified: June 2026.

VA Find Locations

Find VA medical centers, clinics and Vet Centers.

Location: Online VA locator for clinics, hospitals, Vet Centers and other VA offices.
