Practical notes

Use this page well.

Use the crisis line first

If there is any risk of self-harm, violence or immediate danger, call 988 then press 1, text 838255 or call 911.

Do not wait for perfect words

A family member can call too. Say you are worried about a veteran and need help with the next safe step.
