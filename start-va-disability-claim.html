Claims starting point

How to start a VA disability claim.

This is not claims advice. It is a plain-language starting point for finding official VA claim resources and accredited help.

Fast answer: Start at VA.gov, then consider a county veteran service office, Veterans Service Organization or accredited representative if you want help. Vols4Vets does not file or manage claims.

First three steps

  1. Make a list of conditions, dates, deployments, treatment places and symptoms you want to ask about.
  2. Gather existing records you already have, but use official portals or accredited help for private documents.
  3. Use VA.gov or an accredited representative to understand the official claim process.

What to bring

DD214 if available, service treatment records you already have, civilian medical records if requested by an official or accredited helper, VA letters, and a written list of questions.

Common mistakes

  • Sending claim numbers or medical records to Vols4Vets.
  • Assuming a private business is accredited without checking.
  • Rushing a claim without understanding what official evidence is needed.

Safety and privacy note

Vols4Vets is not the VA, not a VSO and not an accredited claims representative. We do not prepare, file, appeal or manage VA claims. Do not send private claim details to Vols4Vets.
