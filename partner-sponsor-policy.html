Partner and Sponsor Policy

  • Sponsors cannot buy verified status.
  • Sponsors cannot control editorial placement.
  • Predatory services are not accepted.
  • Claims-related businesses require extra review.
  • Financial, legal, medical and claims services must show proper licensing or accreditation where applicable.
  • Sponsored placements must be labeled.
  • Veteran-owned does not automatically mean trusted.
