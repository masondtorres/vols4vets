Correction request

Report a broken public link.

Help keep Vols4Vets useful. Send only the public page or link that appears broken. Do not send private records or case details.

Email broken link reportCorrection request
Do not send private information. Do not send Social Security numbers, claim numbers, DD214s, medical records, legal papers, passwords, medical history, legal history or claim evidence.

What to include

  • The Vols4Vets page where you found the broken link.
  • The name of the resource.
  • What happened when you clicked it.
  • A public replacement link if you know one.

What happens next

Vols4Vets may review the public link and update or remove it. Vols4Vets does not handle emergencies, claims, legal issues or medical issues through this page.
