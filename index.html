Simple process

Three steps. No runaround.

  1. Call, email or start with the directory. Tell us the kind of help you need.
  2. Use the right starting point. We point you to the official office, local provider or direct resource.
  3. Verify current details. Eligibility, hours, discounts and programs can change.

What Vols4Vets is

Vols4Vets is an independent veteran-owned LLC based in Sevierville, Tennessee. We help veterans and families find clearer next steps.

What Vols4Vets is not

We are not the VA, a crisis line, a law firm or an accredited claims representative.
