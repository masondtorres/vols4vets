Fastest path

Use the five-question tool if you do not know where to start. It does not ask for private records and it does not submit anything to Vols4Vets.

Safety first: If there is immediate danger, call 911. For veteran crisis support, call 988 then press 1.

Founder note

Built by veterans who live here.

Mason and Jen Torres are United States Air Force veterans based in Sevierville. Vols4Vets was built because a veteran under pressure should not have to dig through ten tabs, dead links and agency language to find the first move.

The site starts local, keeps crisis numbers visible and points to official sources before private services.
